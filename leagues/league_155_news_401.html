River Cats End Fraley's Streak
Saturday, May 16th, 2020
Lady Luck was not on the side of Jake Fraley or his teammates today at Cheney Stadium.

Everything went the way of the Sacramento River Cats, who not only came away with the 2-1 victory over Tacoma, but also held Fraley hitless in 5 at-bats to put an end to his 20-game hitting streak.

"It was a good run," Fraley said afterward. "It would be much easier to take right now if we had won."

So far this year in 21 games Fraley is hitting .463 with 5 home runs, 20 RBIs and 19 runs scored.
