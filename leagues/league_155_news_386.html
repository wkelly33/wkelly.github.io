Injury Brings Season to End for Baird
Friday, May 15th, 2020
The Memphis Redbirds have lost the services of Michael Baird for the rest of this season. He suffered a torn ulnar collateral ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Baird is out for the year.

In season stats Baird shows a 0-1 record in 13 games, 26.1 innings, 34 strikeouts and a 5.47 ERA.
