Stockton Shortstop Nets CAL Award
Monday, May 18th, 2020
So far in his young career, Logan Davidson has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old Stockton Ports shortstop hit at a torrid .458 clip as he collected 11 hits in 24 at-bats, 3 home runs, 12 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

This season in 36 games, Davidson has hit at a .280 clip with 37 hits, 6 home runs, 22 RBIs and 24 runs scored.
