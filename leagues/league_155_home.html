Neuse Honored by Pacific Conference
Monday, May 18th, 2020
25-year-old Sheldon Neuse made a few Las Vegas headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Pacific Conference officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 27 at-bats to put up a .407 average. Neuse collected 5 home runs, 8 RBIs and scored 10 runs.

Currently Neuse is batting .432 with 64 hits and 12 home runs. He has knocked in 26 runs and scored 39 times.
