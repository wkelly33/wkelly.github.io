Reynolds: Big Hit in the Midwest League
Monday, May 18th, 2020
This week Midwest League pitching was a feast fit for a king and Sean Reynolds really made the most of it -- ripping them royally. The talented Clinton hitting machine produced a .526 batting average, 3 home runs, 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 6 RBIs and 7 runs.

That kind of hitting earned him the MID Player of the Week honors.

Reynolds is currently batting .314 with 11 home runs, 33 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has 37 hits in 118 at-bats.
