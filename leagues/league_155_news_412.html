Iowa's Hoerner Gets AC Player of the Week
Monday, May 18th, 2020
One of the league's most exciting young players, Nico Hoerner of the Iowa Cubs earned himself American Conference Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 23-year-old second baseman had a lofty .520 average (13-for-25), collecting 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

This year Hoerner is hitting .352 with 4 home runs.
