MLB News: The All-Star Game Voting Begins
Wednesday, May 20th, 2020
"Vote Early and Vote Often"

While that may not be true for elections, that's the message coming out of the Major League Baseball offices for the upcoming All-Star game, scheduled for Tuesday, July 14th, 2020. The ballots have been released, and everyone can cast your ballot up until a few days before the game, when the final rosters will be released.
Carpenter Hits in 20 Straight Games
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
St. Louis third baseman Matt Carpenter has extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with a 1-for-5 day. However, the Atlanta Braves took the 9-2 victory over the Cardinals at Busch Stadium.

"Disappointing," Carpenter said of the outcome.
Matt Carpenter struck out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, grounded out in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th and struck out in the 9th.

Presently Carpenter is hitting .285 with 5 home runs and 21 RBIs. He has played in 38 games and scored 15 times.
