Osuna Runs Hit String to 20 Straight Games
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
The hot bat of Indians first baseman Jose Osuna continues to churn out hit after hit, game after game. His consecutive-game hitting streak now numbers 20 games after he went 2-for-4 in today's 5-1 loss to the Stripers at Coolray Field.

"I'm trying not to think about it much," he said later. "I don't want to mess with whatever zone I'm in."
Jose Osuna singled in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, singled in the 6th and flied out in the 9th.

To date this season Osuna is hitting .381 with 4 home runs.
