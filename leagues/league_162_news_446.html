River Bandits End Keirsey's Streak
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
It was the best of both worlds for the Quad Cities River Bandits today at Modern Woodmen Park, as they defeated the Cedar Rapids Kernels 16-8 and also snapped DaShawn Keirsey's 21-game hitting streak. The 23-year-old Cedar Rapids center fielder failed to get a hit in 4 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Keirsey, we just wanted to win the game," said River Bandits skipper Ray Hernandez. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Keirsey frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

This season Keirsey is batting at a .325 clip with 3 home runs and 23 RBIs.
