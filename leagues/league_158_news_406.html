Sod Poodles Campusano Smacks 5 Hits
Sunday, May 17th, 2020
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Luis Campusano is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-6 against the Frisco RoughRiders at Dr. Pepper Ballpark today. The monster effort from the 21-year-old catcher helped the Amarillo Sod Poodles take the win, 14-5.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said RoughRiders manager Bobby Wilson. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Luis Campusano singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

This season Campusano is batting .263 with 1 home run.
