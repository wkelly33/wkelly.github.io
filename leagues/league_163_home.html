Injury Brings Season to End for Vilera
Wednesday, May 20th, 2020
Jaison Vilera had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a damaged elbow ligament on 05/19/2020. Today he received word from the Columbia doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

Up to now Vilera has compiled an ERA of 3.86 with a 0-2 mark over 7 starts. He has 24 strikeouts in 30.1 innings and has held opponents to a .246 batting average.
Cropley Sensational, 5-for-6
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
Tyler Cropley did more than his part as the Hagerstown Suns topped the Columbia Fireflies today at Segra Park. The Suns catcher pounded out 5 hits to pace his team to a 19-1 thumping of the Fireflies. Tyler Cropley flied out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Frank Valentino in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This year Cropley has hit 4 home runs and posted a .280 batting average.

Fitzgerald Boosts Hit Streak to 20 Games
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
Tyler Fitzgerald, shortstop for the Augusta GreenJackets, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20 games with 1 hit in his club's 15-2 victory today over the Greenville Drive at SRP Park today.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Greenville manager Ignacio Suarez said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Tyler Fitzgerald singled in the 1st, walked in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, struck out in the 5th and struck out in the 7th.

Looking at his season stats, Fitzgerald has compiled a .333 batting average and a .396 on-base percentage, while hitting 3 home runs and driving in 18 runs.
