No Comeback This Season for Lopez
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
Juan Pablo Lopez had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering shoulder inflammation on 05/16/2020. Today he received word from the Fayetteville doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

This season Lopez has a 1-2 record with a 3.23 ERA.

Carolina League Announces #1 Player
Monday, May 18th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Carolina League, but last week Willy Yahn of the Frederick Keys reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Yahn rocked opposing pitching for a .667 average (12-for-18), 3 home runs and 7 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Yahn is hitting .667 with 12 hits, 3 home runs, 7 RBIs, scored 8 runs and played in 5 games.
