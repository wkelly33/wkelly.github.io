Blankenhorn Picked Best Player
Monday, May 18th, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Travis Blankenhorn was rewarded with the Florida State League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Fort Myers Mighty Mussels left fielder hit .417 while collecting 10 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs and 7 RBIs.

So far this season Blankenhorn is hitting .368 with 7 home runs.
