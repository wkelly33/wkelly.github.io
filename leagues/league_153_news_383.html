Hit Parade: Shaw Pops 5 Hits
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
A jubilant Travis Shaw joined the postgame press conference at Globe Life Field today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Texas Rangers pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Toronto Blue Jays first baseman, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-5 hitting performance, which led the Blue Jays to a 7-6 win.

"He made it look easy," said Chris Woodward, manager of the Rangers. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Travis Shaw singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Juan Nicasio in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Thus far this year Shaw has hit .224 with 6 home runs and 15 RBIs.
