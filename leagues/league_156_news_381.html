Bowie-Harrisburg Fight Results in Suspensions
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
Jomar Reyes of Bowie and Seth Romero of Harrisburg were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. Reyes will have to miss 4 games for his part in the brawl, while Romero was given 9 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

Batting .229 to date, Reyes has 2 home runs and 12 RBIs.

So far this season Romero has a 2-1 record, 7 starts, 41.2 innings, 38 strikeouts and a 3.24 ERA.
