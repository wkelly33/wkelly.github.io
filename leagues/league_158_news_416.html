TEX Trophy Goes to Santana of Tulsa
Monday, May 18th, 2020
It was announced today that Tulsa third baseman Cristian Santana had received the Texas League Player of the Week Award for his impressive play last week.

All week long he was terrific at the plate and won the honor with a .480 BA (12-for-25), 2 home runs and 10 RBIs.

Santana on the season is hitting .371 with 7 home runs. His stats include 36 RBIs and 18 runs scored.
