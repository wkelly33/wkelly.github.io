Baltimore Orioles Ship Givens to Cincinnati
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
According to BNN there were mixed emotions by the players involved in the trade between the Baltimore Orioles and the Cincinnati Reds. As part of the deal, the Orioles swapped 30-year-old RHP Mychal Givens to the Reds getting 24-year-old minor league RHP Ryan Campbell in return. The players indicated it was good to get a fresh new start in another city, but it was difficult to leave your friends on the old team.


This year Givens has made 18 relief appearances, pitched 16 innings, and fashioned a 1-3 record with a 7.88 ERA. He has recorded 9 saves, struck out 17, and allowed opposing clubs to hit .329 when he is on the hill.
