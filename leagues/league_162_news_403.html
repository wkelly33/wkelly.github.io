Keirsey Ups Hit Streak to 20 Consecutive Games
Saturday, May 16th, 2020
With 1 hit at Veterans Memorial Stadium today, DaShawn Keirsey added another game to his hitting streak -- the Cedar Rapids center fielder has now hit safely for 20 games in a row. But the hitting feat wasn't enough for the Kernels today, as they fell, 16-5, to the Kane County Cougars.

"Frankly, I don't care about hits," Kane County manager Vince Harrison said later. "I'll let him get 4 hits in 4 at-bats if it means we get a win."
DaShawn Keirsey was hit by a pitch in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, struck out in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

Thus far this season Keirsey is hitting .336 with 3 home runs, 20 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has 50 hits in 149 at-bats and has played in 34 games.
