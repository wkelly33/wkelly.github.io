Star of the Week Honored in NL
Monday, May 18th, 2020
Freddy Galvis is respected by most Cincinnati Reds fans for his no-frills style of play. The 30-year-old shortstop went about his business with vigor last week to grab the National League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .520 average (13-for-25) with 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

This season Galvis is hitting .314 with 5 home runs.
