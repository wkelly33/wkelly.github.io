Walls Smacks 5 Hits for Bulls
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Taylor Walls is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-5 against the Gwinnett Stripers at Durham Bulls Athletic Park today. The monster effort from the 23-year-old second baseman helped the Durham Bulls take the win, 7-4.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Stripers manager Damon Berryhill. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Taylor Walls doubled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

For the year Walls is batting .500 with 1 home run, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
