Hit Parade: Soto Gets Cycle
Washington Nationals left fielder Juan Soto hit for the cycle today at Nationals Park but his great game was soured as the Chicago Cubs went home victors by a score of 10-5.

He told reporters in the postgame press conference that he did not know what he had achieved until he was informed by the Washington PR staff following the game.

"As long as the game is going, it's hard for me to think about anything but what is on the field," he said. "It's a nice accomplishment, I guess. I wish it had been under better circumstances."

Juan Soto singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, struck out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Rowan Wick in the 7th and hit an RBI triple in the 9th.
