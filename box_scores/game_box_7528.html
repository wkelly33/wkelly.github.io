5-Hit Barrage by Garcia
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Luis Garcia of the Fredericksburg Nationals. Garcia went on a tear at New Fredericksburg Ballpark and cracked hit after hit off Frederick pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 19-year-old second baseman had a career 5-for-5 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Garcia's personal accolades, the Frederick Keys walked off the field with the 8-4 win.

Luis Garcia singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Matthias Dietz in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

For the year Garcia is batting .309 with 4 home runs.
