Schrock Slaps 5 Hits
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Max Schrock, it came around today.

The 25-year-old second baseman scattered hits all over AutoZone Park to pace the Memphis Redbirds to a 6-5 victory over the Omaha Storm Chasers.

Omaha skipper Brian Poldberg told reporters in the press room that "Schrock deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Schrock hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Max Schrock singled in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.
